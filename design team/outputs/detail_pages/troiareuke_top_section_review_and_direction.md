# TROIAREUKE Top Section Review And Direction

## 1. Team Review

### Overall assessment

The draft has a good strategic direction.
It clearly frames the set as an entry-point routine for first-time TROIAREUKE customers.
The main opportunity is to sharpen the language so it feels more premium, more concise, and more visually aligned with the reference mood.

### What works

- `Bring your spa home` is consistent with the reference image and kit naming.
- The message of `first-time entry home aesthetics` is easy to understand.
- The `2-step care system` angle is practical and suitable for a detail page opener.

### What should be improved

- Some lines are slightly explanatory and long for the top section.
- `처음 만나는`, `처음의 스킨케어` type phrasing repeats the same idea.
- `민낯 연구소 Pick!` feels more promotional than premium.
- The reference mood is not cheerful or casual; it is controlled, clinical, gift-like, and elevated.
- The top section should lead with a stronger premium promise before moving into explanation.

## 2. Reference Mood Summary

Based on the reference images, the visual mood should follow these cues:

- Deep navy background
- Cool blue halo or glow
- Silver metallic reflections
- Transparent liquid and clear glass texture
- Strong center-focused product hero composition
- Premium gift-kit feeling rather than crowded commerce-style layout

## 3. Recommended Tone

- Minimal
- Premium
- Clean
- Controlled
- Aesthetic-clinic inspired

Avoid:

- Overly cute expressions
- Loud event-style wording
- Too many selling points in the first frame

## 9. Top Section Layout Guide

### Frame 01: Hero opener

- Message: `Bring your spa home`
- Layout: left-aligned text block on upper-left, product hero on right or center-right
- Background: deep navy gradient
- Visual accent: cool blue glow or soft ring light reference
- Type:
  - English line: `Caption 16px`, tracking `-20`
  - Korean H1: `52-56px`, semibold, tracking `-40`
  - Sub line: `20px`, regular, tracking `-20`

### Frame 02: Why this kit

- Message: first-time TROIAREUKE start routine
- Keep only one short paragraph and three point bullets
- Use more negative space than icons
- Suggested heading: `트로이아르케를 시작하는 가장 간결한 방법`

### Frame 03: 2-step routine

- Message: cleanse, then ampoule
- Visual: product pair or three-step kit lineup with line divider
- Suggested heading: `정돈하고, 채우는 2-Step 루틴`

### Frame 04: Who it is for

- Message: first-time users and routine seekers
- Layout: three short recommendation bullets
- Keep this section quieter than the hero

## 10. Production Notes

- Keep the first frame text count low
- Do not place all points, targets, and timing copy in one slide
- Use the product pack shot or ring-light shot as the hero visual reference
- Preserve wide margins inside the `860px` safe area
- Use white or very light gray typography over navy
- Use lavender only as a small accent taken from the cleanser cap
