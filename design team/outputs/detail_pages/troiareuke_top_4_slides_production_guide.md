# TROIAREUKE Top 4 Slides Production Guide

## Project Direction

- Format: `1080 x 1350px`
- Safe width: `860px`
- Font: `Pretendard`
- Tone: `Minimal / Premium / Home Aesthetics`
- Base palette:
  - Deep Navy `#09162D`
  - Royal Blue Glow `#144D9C`
  - Silver Gray `#C8CDD6`
  - Soft White `#F5F7FA`
  - Accent Lavender `#8D7CE6`

## Slide 01

### Core message

Bring your spa home

### Copy

- Caption:
  - `TROIAREUKE Home Aesthetics Kit`
- H1:
  - `처음 만나는 트로이아르케,`
  - `집에서 시작하는 홈 에스테틱`
- Body:
  - `에스테틱의 시작을 더 간결하고 정제된 루틴으로.`

### Layout guide

- Text block aligned left, placed in upper-left area
- Product hero image placed center-right
- Keep the hero large and clean with wide empty margins
- Use one ring-light or glow accent only
- Do not add bullet points on this slide

### Type guide

- Caption: `16px Regular`, tracking `-20`
- H1: `54px Semibold`, tracking `-40`, width `98%`
- Body: `20px Light`, tracking `-20`

### Visual direction

- Use the premium kit or three-product hero image
- Background should be deep navy with a soft blue glow
- Use white typography and avoid decorative graphic elements

## Slide 02

### Core message

처음 시작하는 고객을 위한 스타트 루틴

### Copy

- H2:
  - `트로이아르케를 시작하는`
  - `가장 간결한 방법`
- Body:
  - `트로이아르케를 처음 시작하는 고객을 위해`
  - `가장 먼저 필요한 루틴만 담았습니다.`
- Point 01:
  - `처음 시작하는 트로이아르케 스타트 솔루션`
- Point 02:
  - `집에서 완성하는 에스테틱 2-Step 루틴`
- Point 03:
  - `클렌저와 앰플로 정돈하는 홈 케어 시스템`

### Layout guide

- Left-aligned headline and body in upper-left
- Three points stacked vertically with large spacing
- Use thin divider lines or small square markers
- Keep product image secondary and faded in the lower-right or background

### Type guide

- H2: `36px Semibold`, tracking `-40`
- Body: `19px Regular`, tracking `-20`
- Points: `18px Semibold`, tracking `-20`

### Visual direction

- Use a cropped kit image or subtle pack-shot shadow
- The layout should feel editorial, not commercial
- Leave generous space around each point

## Slide 03

### Core message

정돈하고, 채우는 2-Step 루틴

### Copy

- H2:
  - `정돈하고, 채우는`
  - `홈 에스테틱 2-Step`
- Step 01 Title:
  - `STEP 1. Oil Cut Cleansing`
- Step 01 Body:
  - `피부를 맑고 개운하게 정돈하는`
  - `클렌징 스타트 단계`
- Step 02 Title:
  - `STEP 2. Healing Cocktail Ampoule`
- Step 02 Body:
  - `수분감 있는 피부 바탕을 차분히 채워주는`
  - `앰플 케어 단계`
- Caption:
  - `매일 이어질수록 더 탄탄해지는 트로이아르케 루틴`

### Layout guide

- Headline at upper-left
- Two-step structure below with clear vertical rhythm
- Product pair or trio arranged on right half
- Use a thin line, column break, or numbered markers to separate steps
- Keep each step to two short lines maximum

### Type guide

- H2: `34px Semibold`, tracking `-40`
- Step title: `22px Semibold`, tracking `-20`
- Step body: `18px Regular`, tracking `-20`
- Caption: `15px Light`, tracking `-20`

### Visual direction

- Show cleanser and ampoule clearly
- Transparent liquid texture and reflective material are welcome
- Do not add too many icons

## Slide 04

### Core message

이런 분께 추천합니다

### Copy

- H2:
  - `이런 분께 추천합니다`
- Recommend 01:
  - `어떤 제품부터 시작해야 할지 고민되는 분`
- Recommend 02:
  - `집에서도 정돈된 홈케어 루틴을 만들고 싶은 분`
- Recommend 03:
  - `트로이아르케를 처음 경험하는 분`
- Closing line:
  - `매일의 루틴을 더 정제된 시작으로 바꾸어 보세요.`

### Layout guide

- Left-aligned headline and recommendation list
- Three recommendation lines with wide spacing
- Closing line set lower with lighter weight
- Product image can sit quietly on the far right or bottom-right

### Type guide

- H2: `34px Semibold`, tracking `-40`
- Recommendation lines: `20px Regular`, tracking `-20`
- Closing line: `16px Light`, tracking `-20`

### Visual direction

- Use a calm product still-life image
- Reduce glow strength compared with Slide 01
- Make this feel quieter and more informative

## Production Summary

- `Slide 01`: brand-first hero
- `Slide 02`: why this kit
- `Slide 03`: 2-step routine
- `Slide 04`: recommended for

## Final Design Notes

- One slide should communicate one message only
- Keep all text within the `860px` safe width system
- Default to left alignment across all four slides
- Use lavender only as a very small accent drawn from the cleanser cap
- Use navy and cool blue as the dominant visual mood
- Prioritize premium still-life balance over aggressive sales composition
